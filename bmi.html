how to find out your ideal weight?

body mass index

To determine your ideal weight, the Body Mass Index (BMI) helps assess whether your weight is appropriate for your height or how near it is to a healthy range.

Worried about excess weight?

There is a reliable and science based method to find out if you have excess weight.

Find out your actual BMI

Calculate your BMI by dividing your weight by your height squared. You don't need to do the math manually, just use the online calculator provided below.
